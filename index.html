Biography

I'm James Maverick R. Barnayha, a BSIT student majoring in Web Technology at the University of the Cordilleras. Born on October 26, 2006, in Calapacuan, Subic, Zambales.

I am interested in web designing, UI/UX, and video editing. I find it amusing whenever I create a website that allows me to manipulate its content and design. Beyond web development, I have a deep interest in video editing and motion graphics.

During my elementary years, I attended Mount Carmel School of San Luis and graduated in 2019 with honors, then for my secondary I attended Aurora National Science High School, graduated in 2025 with Outstanding Performance awards in Communication Arts and Media Arts (Filipino category).

I'm currently pursuing my degree at the University of the Cordilleras. I aspire to become a professional web developer and designer who creates visually stunning, user-friendly websites that allow me to blend my ability to code with my creative and artistic vision.
