My Story

I'm James Maverick R. Barnayha, a BSIT student majoring in Web Technology at the University of the Cordilleras. Born on October 26, 2006, in Calapacuan, Subic, Zambales.

I am interested in web designing, UI/UX, and video editing. I find it ammusing whenever I create a website that allows me to manipulate its content and design. Beyond web development, I have a deep interest in video editing and motion graphics, allowing me to blend my ability to code and my artistic vision.

During my elementary years, I attended at Mount Carmel School of San Luis and graduated in 2019 with honors, then for my secondary I attended Aurora National Science High School, graduating in 2025 with Outstanding Performance awards in Communication Arts and Media Arts (Filipino category).

I'm Currently pursuing my degree at the University of the Cordilleras, I aspire to become a professional web developer and designer who creates visually stunning, user-friendly websites that push the boundaries of what's possible on the web.
