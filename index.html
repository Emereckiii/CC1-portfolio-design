About Me

My Story

I'm James Maverick R. Barnayha, a BSIT student majoring in Web Technology at the University of the Cordilleras. Born on October 26, 2006, in Calapacuan, Subic, Zambales, I'm passionate about creating innovative digital experiences.

My journey in technology began with a fascination for web development and design. I thrive on learning new technologies and bringing creative ideas to life through code. Beyond web development, I have a deep interest in video editing and motion graphics, allowing me to blend technical expertise with artistic vision.

I graduated from Mount Carmel School of San Luis in 2019 with honors, then attended Aurora National Science High School, graduating in 2025 with Outstanding Performance awards in Communication Arts and Media Arts (Filipino category).

Currently pursuing my degree at the University of the Cordilleras, I aspire to become a professional web developer and designer who creates visually stunning, user-friendly websites that push the boundaries of what's possible on the web.
